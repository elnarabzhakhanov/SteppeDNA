Problem

Variants of Uncertain Significance (VUS) in DNA repair genes prevent accurate cancer risk assessment for patients and clinicians.

Existing predictors (REVEL, CADD) have limited coverage and poor performance on non-BRCA2 HR genes — PALB2, RAD51C, RAD51D.

Can an ensemble ML model accurately classify missense variants across 5 HR genes?

Conclusions
  1. SteppeDNA achieves AUC 0.978 across 5 HR genes with 100% variant coverage
  2. Population-frequency augmentation is critical for data-scarce genes
  3. Per-gene reliability tiers ensure honest, transparent reporting
  4. Web platform delivers SHAP explanations, ACMG evidence, and 3D protein visualization

Future work: Larger ESM-2 models (650M), prospective clinical validation, Central Asian population data
